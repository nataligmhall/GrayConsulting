The story

Built for someone
I love.

Someone I love needed a better way to manage their daily wellness — mood, sleep, medications, and habits. The apps that existed were either too clinical, too cluttered, or paywalled. So I built one from scratch. This is that app.

Why this exists

One person. No funding. Just something that needed to exist.

I spent months building this, learning as I went, rebuilding things that didn't feel right, and constantly asking one question: would they actually use this every day? Not "is this a good product" but "does this feel like something made for a real human."

Managing daily health means logging things you'd rather forget — bad sleep, missed meds, a hard mood day. The friction of that has to be as low as possible, and the app has to feel safe. No judgement. No ads. No company reading your entries.

The penguin was her idea.

What it does

Mood journal

Daily check-ins with mood, brain climate, and free-form notes. Spot patterns over time.

Habit & goal streaks

Set personal goals, mark daily completions, and build consecutive-day streaks.

Stability calendar

Visual month view showing your mood history — good days, hard days, and trends.

Sleep & meds tracking

Log sleep quality and medication adherence. See 14-day irritability and sleep trends.

Smart reminders

Daily check-in notifications with quick time presets. Never forget to log.

Color themes

Pick a palette that feels like you with themes like Arctic Moss, Aurora Borealis, Midnight Sun, and more.
